About

I'm a data scientist and electronic engineer, with a postgraduate specialization in artificial intelligence. My work combines data analysis, statistical modeling and software development.

I develop machine learning and deep learning models to optimize processes and support decision-making, and I handle the full flow, from preparing the data to the model in production. I work independently and within cross-disciplinary teams, turning business problems into technical solutions.

I approach problems with an engineering mindset: I structure what's complex, base decisions on data, and validate each step before taking it as final. I write clear, maintainable code, and I would rather deliver reliable results than rushed conclusions.

I work fluently in Spanish and English, have an intermediate level of French, and I'm studying Japanese. My experience spans different fields of engineering and development, which gives me a broad perspective when tackling new problems. I want to join teams where technical rigor and quality come first.
